Procedure for deleting 1C:Enterprise application versions

1. In the search field on the taskbar or in the Start menu, enter control panel and select "Control Panel".

2. Click Programs > Programs and Features.

3. Right-click the line with 1C:Enterprise 8 %1 with a platform version you want to delete. Select Uninstall or Uninstall/Change. After that, follow the instructions on the screen.
